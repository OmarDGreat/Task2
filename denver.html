About Denver

Welcome to the Mile-High City! Denver, the capital of Colorado, is a vibrant metropolis situated at an elevation of exactly one mile above sea level. Known for its panoramic views of the Rocky Mountains, Denver offers a perfect blend of urban sophistication and outdoor adventure.

Explore the cultural attractions in downtown Denver, including the Denver Art Museum and the historic Union Station. For sports enthusiasts, catch a game at the iconic Coors Field or Mile High Stadium. Denver's diverse neighborhoods provide unique experiences, from the trendy RiNo Arts District to the historic charm of Capitol Hill.

With a thriving food scene, craft breweries, and numerous parks, Denver is a city that caters to a wide range of interests. Whether you're into hiking, museums, or sampling local cuisine, Denver has something for everyone.

Additional Information

Did you know that Denver has more than 200 parks within the city limits? City Park, with its beautiful lakes and the Denver Zoo, offers a perfect escape for nature lovers. Denver is also famous for its annual Great American Beer Festival, attracting beer enthusiasts from around the world.
