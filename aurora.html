About Aurora

Aurora, a vibrant city located just east of Denver, is known for its diverse communities and rich cultural offerings. As the third most populous city in Colorado, Aurora boasts a mix of residential neighborhoods, parks, and cultural attractions.

Explore the Aurora Reservoir for outdoor activities such as fishing, boating, and hiking, or visit the Aurora History Museum to learn about the city's past. The Southlands Shopping Center offers a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options for residents and visitors alike.

Aurora's commitment to green spaces and community events makes it a welcoming place to live and explore. With easy access to Denver and the Denver International Airport, Aurora serves as a gateway to the broader Denver metropolitan area.

Additional Information

Fun fact: Aurora is home to the Stanley Marketplace, a unique urban market housed in a former aviation building. The marketplace features a mix of local businesses, restaurants, and craft breweries. Aurora also hosts the annual Global Fest, celebrating the city's cultural diversity with food, music, and performances from around the world.
